Ethiopian Regions

Diversity in Unity

Amhara Region

Home to Lake Tana (source of Blue Nile) and Simien Mountains National Park.

Capital: Bahir Dar

Oromia Region

Largest region by area, known for coffee production and Bale Mountains.

Capital: Adama

Tigray Region

Historic region with ancient Axumite civilization and rock-hewn churches.

Capital: Mekelle
