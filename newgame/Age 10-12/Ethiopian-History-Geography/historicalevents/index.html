1270

Solomonic Dynasty Begins

Yekuno Amlak overthrows the Zagwe dynasty, claiming descent from King Solomon and Queen of Sheba.

1896

Battle of Adwa

Ethiopia defeats Italian forces, becoming the first African nation to resist European colonialism.

1936-1941

Italian Occupation

Brief period of Italian rule under Mussolini, ending with Ethiopian resistance and Allied support.
