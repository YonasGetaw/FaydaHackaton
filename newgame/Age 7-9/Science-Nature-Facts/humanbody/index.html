The Amazing Human Body

Skeletal System

Your body has 206 bones that work together to help you stand and move.

The smallest bone is in your ear and is as tiny as a grain of rice!

Digestive System

Food travels through a 9-meter long tube called the digestive tract.

Your stomach acid is strong enough to dissolve metal!

Circulatory System

Your heart pumps blood through 96,000 km of blood vessels - that's enough to go around Earth twice!

Your heart beats about 100,000 times every day!

Your body is amazing! Take good care of it.
