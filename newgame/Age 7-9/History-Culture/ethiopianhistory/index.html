Ethiopian History

980 BC

Queen of Sheba

The Queen of Sheba visited King Solomon in Jerusalem. Their son Menelik I started Ethiopia's Solomonic dynasty.

4th Century

Christianity in Ethiopia

Ethiopia became one of the first Christian countries in the world when King Ezana converted.

12th Century

Lalibela Churches

King Lalibela built 11 amazing rock-hewn churches that are still standing today!

1896

Battle of Adwa

Ethiopia defeated Italy at Adwa, becoming the only African country to resist European colonization.

Ethiopia has a proud history spanning thousands of years!
