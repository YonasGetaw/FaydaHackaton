Good Manners

Magic Words

Always say "please" when asking for something and "thank you" when you receive it. These are called magic words!

Sharing is Caring

Share your toys and snacks with friends. It makes everyone happy and helps you make more friends.

Good Listening

When others are speaking, listen carefully without interrupting. Wait for your turn to talk.

Remember: Good manners make you a great friend and a wonderful person!
